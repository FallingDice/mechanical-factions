##⚙️ JunkTech Industry (WIP)
"Unleash the power of salvaged technology!"

JunkTech Industry is a faction expansion mod for Mindustry. Build, recycle, and conquer with a unique army of scrap-metal units and specialized structures.


###🚀 Key Features
- New Mechanical Faction: Introducing the 'JunkTech' faction with its own distinct visual style and gameplay.

- 10+ New Units: A diverse lineup of mechanical units salvaged from the scrap heap.

- Specialized Turrets: Defend your core with turrets featuring unique firing patterns and mechanics.

- Scrap-based Tech: Focus on recycling and heavy industrial warfare.


###⚠️ Status & Issues
This mod is currently Work In Progress (WIP). I am developing this project solo to explore Mindustry modding.

- Known Issues: Currently investigating a Stackoverflowerror and some initial stability issues reported in the Issues tab.

- Contributions: I am currently focusing on personal development, so I am not accepting Pull Requests at this time. However, feedback via Issues is always welcome!
